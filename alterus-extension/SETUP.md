# Alterus Extension — Setup in 3 Minutes

## Step 1 — Install (30 seconds)

1. Download the `alterus-extension` folder
2. Open Chrome → go to `chrome://extensions`
3. Turn on **Developer mode** (top right)
4. Click **Load unpacked** → select the folder
5. Done ✦ appears on the right side of Gmail/Outlook/Slack

---

## Step 2 — Set Up Your Profile (1 minute)

Click the **✦ icon** in Chrome toolbar (top right):

1. Enter your **name** (required)
2. Enter your **role** and **company**
3. Enter your **key contacts** — comma separated
   e.g. `Jason Wong, Sarah Chen, Mike Lee`
4. The **Server URL** is already filled in
5. Click **Save Profile**
6. Click **Test Connection** — should show ✅

---

## Step 3 — Connect Gmail (30 seconds)

1. Open **Gmail** in Chrome
2. Click the **✦ tab** on the right edge
3. Click **Connect Gmail**
4. Sign in with Google → approve
5. Alterus learns from your last 60 sent emails

---

## Step 4 — Use It

1. Open any email in Gmail, Outlook, Slack, or Teams
2. Click the **✦ tab** on the right
3. Choose tone: Direct / Balanced / Diplomatic
4. Click **Draft Reply**
5. Edit if needed → click **↗ Insert** or **⎘ Copy**
6. Hit send as normal

---

## Platforms Supported

- ✅ Gmail (mail.google.com)
- ✅ Outlook Web (outlook.office.com)
- ✅ Slack Web (app.slack.com)
- ✅ Microsoft Teams Web (teams.microsoft.com)

---

## Tips

- The more you use it, the better it gets
- When browsing **Sent Items** in Outlook, it silently learns your style
- 👍 👎 buttons on every draft — your feedback improves future drafts
- Change tone per email — Direct for quick replies, Diplomatic for sensitive ones
